About Us

MISSION:
Next Gen Youth Leaders (NGYL) is a non-profit (501c3) organization committed to nurturing young leaders within the Asian American community. Our mission is to encourage youth engagement in recreational and educational projects, enhancing their experiences as active community members. Our primary goal is to foster a strong youth leadership presence that can effectively serve and support our youth, families, and fellow citizens in the community.

NGYL aims to provide youths the opportunity to gain leadership experiences, skills that will equip the future generation with the necessary communication and awareness skills to better the community and the world.
